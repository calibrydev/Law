Labor Law

Employment law

Criminal law

Criminal law is the body of law that relates to crime. It prescribes conduct perceived as threatening, harmful, health, safety, and moral welfare of people inclusive of one's self.

Family law

Family law deals with family matters and domestic relations, including marriage and civil partnerships, the termination of relationships and child law..

Corporate law

Corporate law governs the functioning of companies; from how they are formed to the transactions they are permitted to engage in.

Business law

Business law (sometimes called “commercial” or “corporate” law) is a broad term used to describe lawyers who work on issues that impact businesses..
